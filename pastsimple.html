Learn Past Simple! 📚

Master the past simple tense and learn about actions that happened in the past!

How to Use Past Simple ⏮️

✅ Regular Verbs

Add -ed: work → worked
I worked yesterday.
Add -d: live → lived
She lived in Paris.

🔄 Irregular Verbs

go → went: They went home.
eat → ate: We ate pizza.
see → saw: I saw a movie.
have → had: He had lunch.

❌ Negative Form

didn't + base verb
I didn't work yesterday.
She didn't go to school.

❓ Question Form

Did + subject + base verb?
Did you work yesterday?
Did she go to school?

Story Examples 📖

Yesterday's Adventure

Yesterday, I woke up early.

I ate breakfast with my family.

Then we went to the park.

We played games and had fun!

In the evening, I watched a movie.

Last Weekend

Last Saturday, it was sunny.

My friends came to visit me.

We made sandwiches for lunch.

After that, we took photos.

We didn't want the day to end!

Grammar Tips! 💡

📚

Learn Irregular Verbs

Practice common irregular verbs - they don't follow the -ed pattern!

⏰

Finished Actions

Use past simple for actions that started and finished in the past.

🔍

Questions & Negatives

Remember to use "did" + base verb for questions and negatives.

📖

Tell Stories

Past simple is perfect for telling stories about what happened!

Keep practicing and master Past Simple! 📚✨
