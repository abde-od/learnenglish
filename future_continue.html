Learn Future Continuous! 🔮

Master the future continuous tense and learn to talk about actions that will be in progress in the future!

How to Use Future Continuous 🔄

✅ Positive Form

will + be + verb + ing
I will be working tomorrow.
She will be reading at 8 PM.
They will be playing football.

❌ Negative Form

will not + be + verb + ing
I won't be working tonight.
She won't be reading.
They won't be playing.

❓ Question Form

Will + subject + be + verb + ing?
Will you be working tomorrow?
Will she be reading?

🎯 When to Use

• Actions in progress at a future time
• Future arrangements/plans
• Polite questions about future plans
• Predictions about ongoing future actions

Grammar Tips! 💡

🔮

Future in Progress

Use future continuous for actions that will be happening at a specific future time.

📅

Polite Questions

"Will you be using this?" sounds more polite than "Will you use this?"

⏰

Time References

Often used with time expressions: "at 3 PM", "this time tomorrow", "next week".

🎯

Predictions

Great for predicting what will be happening: "It will be raining tomorrow."
