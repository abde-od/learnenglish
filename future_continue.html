Grammar Tips! üí°

üîÆ

Future in Progress

Use future continuous for actions that will be happening at a specific future time.

üìÖ

Polite Questions

"Will you be using this?" sounds more polite than "Will you use this?"

‚è∞

Time References

Often used with time expressions: "at 3 PM", "this time tomorrow", "next week".

üéØ

Predictions

Great for predicting what will be happening: "It will be raining tomorrow."
