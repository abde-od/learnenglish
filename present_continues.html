Grammar Tips! üí°

‚ö°

Actions in Progress

Use present continuous for actions that are happening right now or around now.

üîÑ

Temporary vs Permanent

Present continuous is for temporary actions, not permanent states or habits.

üö´

Stative Verbs

Don't use continuous with stative verbs like know, like, want, need, have (possession).

üìÖ

Future Arrangements

Use present continuous for planned future events: "We're meeting tomorrow."
